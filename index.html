About Me

I am an emerging documentary filmmaker who is passionate about telling stories centered on human rights, social justice, and the courageous people and communities who fight back in the face of inequity.

I also consider myself an adventure filmmaker - taking my camera with me everywhere from sandy beaches to snow capped mountains. I love hiking and have recently done Te Araroa - South Island, where I documented my (mis)adventures on the trail! I aim to tell personal and reflective stories that serve to catalyze change and inspire people to act.

Films

Women Rising (2023)

Women Rising (2023) is a documentary series which tells the stories of brave and inspiring Iranian women who are speaking up and demanding change for women in Iran. The first episode highlights Hanna, one of the key activists behind the Women Life Freedom movement in Wellington, New Zealand and her story of fighting back in the face of gender oppression.
